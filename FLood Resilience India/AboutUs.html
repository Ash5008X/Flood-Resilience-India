About   Us   -   Building   a   Resilient   India,   Together

Flood Resilience India -

Flood Resilience India (FRI) is a comprehensive platform designed to mitigating the devasting impacts of flooding across India. By providing real-time flood information, essential resources, and community engagement, we aim to empower individuals and society with the knowledge and assests needed to prepare for, respond to, and recover from floods effectively. Our Initiative leverages advanced technology to visualize flood risks, identify vulnerabe area and disseminate critical information. By working together, we can build a more resilient India.

Our Platform -

Here, we provide an interactive flood map plot to identify area at high risk of flooding. This tool enables one to take necessary precautions and make informed descisions to safeguard thier lives and property. In addtion to it we have:

  • Community Sharing Features: A space for users to share their experiences and stories, fostering a sense of community and collective strength.
  • Resource Library: A comprehensive collection of flood-related information, including safety tips, emergency contact numbers, and more.
  • Training and Resources: Access to workshops, online training, and other resources to enhance flood preparedness.
  • Knowledge Repository: Access to past articles and case studies on floods, offering valuable insights and lessons learned from previous events.
  • Support : A comprehensive list of NGOs, organizations and Government Schemes ready to provide support and resources during crises.

Our Mission -

To empower communities in India to build resilience against floods and minimize the impact of these devastating natural disasters.

  • Enhancing Preparedness
  • Faciliating Response and Recovery
  • Reducing Losses
  • Promoting Sustainable Development

How we help ?

A flood-resilient India where communities are well-prepared, respond effectively, and recover swiftly from flood disasters.

  • Reduced Loss of Life and Property: By providing timely warnings and empowering communities to take action.
  • Enhanced Anticipation: By equipping communities with the knowledge and tools to mitigate flood risks.
  • Effective Response: By facilitating coordinated and efficient response efforts.
  • Swift Recovery: By supporting communities in rebuilding their lives and livelihoods.
